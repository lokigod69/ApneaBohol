Our Story

Apnea Bohol was founded with a passion for freediving and a deep connection to the beautiful waters of Bohol, Philippines. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the transformative experience of freediving in one of the world's most breathtaking locations.

Located in the heart of Panglao Island, our freediving center offers professional education, training, and experiences for all levels - from complete beginners to advanced freedivers seeking to deepen their practice.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach to freediving, combining technical training with yoga, breathing techniques, and a profound respect for the marine environment that surrounds us.
