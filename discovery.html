Discovery Freedive Program

Dive into the world of freediving with our Discovery Freedive Program at Apnea Bohol! Designed for beginners, this half-day program offers a comprehensive introduction to the essentials of freediving, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience in the water.

What You Will Learn

  • Basics of Freediving
  • Safety and Relaxation Techniques
  • Breathing Exercises
  • Equipment and Gears/Short Fins
  • Dry Static Apnea
  • Line Dive Training
  • Basic Finning Techniques
  • Proper Duckdiving

What's Included

  • Theory Class & Pool Session
  • Open Water Session
  • Resort Fees
  • Environmental Fee
  • Coach Fee
  • Photos and Videos to Capture Your Experience

*Note: Gears are not included in the package, so please bring your own or arrange for rentals separately.

Duration & Cost

The Discovery Freedive Program lasts for half a day, giving you ample time to grasp the fundamentals and practice in both pool and open water settings. The total cost for this immersive experience is PHP 4,500.

Join us at Apnea Bohol to discover the thrill of freediving in a safe, supportive environment. Book your spot today and take your first breath-hold dive into this incredible sport!
