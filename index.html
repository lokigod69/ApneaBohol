About Apnea Bohol

Apnea Bohol was founded by passionate freedivers with a mission to share the transformative power of freediving with the world. We believe that freediving is not just a sport, but a way to connect with yourself and nature on a deeper level.

Our team of experienced instructors is dedicated to providing the highest quality education in a safe and supportive environment.
