Our Freediving Excursions

Balicasag Island Adventure

Join us for an unforgettable day at Balicasag Island, a gem off Panglao's coast! Our tour kicks off with a serene boat ride at dawn, chasing playful dolphins as the sun rises. Once we dock, you'll snorkel in the marine sanctuary, swimming alongside majestic sea turtles and vibrant fish darting through colorful coral gardens.

The crystal-clear waters make every moment feel like a dive into an aquarium. After, relax on the white-sand beach or explore the island's charm—it's the perfect intro to freediving with Apnea Bohol's Wave 1 spirit!

Napaling Sardines Fundive

Dive into the magic of Napaling Reef, Panglao's crown jewel for freedivers! This fun dive takes you to a thriving 25-meter coral wall, where massive schools of sardines shimmer like silver clouds against the vivid reef.

The crystal waters reveal a kaleidoscope of marine life—clownfish, jacks, and more—making every breath a thrill. Perfect for beginners, this excursion blends the joy of freediving with nature's spectacle, embodying the Wave 1 vibe of discovery and wonder. You'll leave mesmerized by the sardines' dance!

Doljo Point

Experience the thrill of Doljo Point, a hidden treasure for freedivers! This exciting excursion takes you to a dramatic drop-off where the reef plunges into the deep blue. Perfect for those ready to push their limits, you'll descend alongside vibrant coral formations teeming with marine life.

Look out for sea turtles, reef sharks, and schools of jackfish that often patrol these waters. With depths ranging from 5 to 30+ meters, Doljo offers challenges for every level while maintaining the safety and support that defines Apnea Bohol.

Book This Excursion

Arco Point

Discover the enchanting underwater world of Arco Point, a freediver's paradise! This special site features a magnificent natural arch formation and an underwater cave system that creates a magical play of light and shadow beneath the waves.

With depths ranging from shallow reef flats to 20+ meter drop-offs, Arco Point offers something for every freediver. The biodiversity here is stunning—nudibranchs, frogfish, and even the occasional sea snake make appearances, rewarding those who dive with patience and awareness.
